
The most ancient Sanskrit epic poem estimated to have been composed by sage Valmiki about 5000 B.C the Ramayana describes the life of Sri Rama. The epic is a storehouse of spiritual wisdom and has helped to shape the ideals and character of the Indian people since antiquity. The present work is a beautifully condensed retelling of the Ramayana in simple Englishwhich eaplains the adventures of Rama Sita Hanuman and arch-villain Ravana. This inexpensive edition tells the story in an abridged format.
