Publishing Date: Feb-2012
Sri Ramakrishna's 175th Birth Anniversary Publication.

Swami Brahmananda (1863-1922) the spiritual son of Sri Ramakrishna was the first president of the Ramakrishna Math and Mission. He held in high esteem for his spiritual attainments.

This booklet contains some of his religious precepts compiled by Sri P Sheshadri Aiyar of Kerala who closely associated with him at various places. The text of this booklet is taken from Swami Brahmananda As We Saw Him (p 549 - 557) published by Sri Ramakrishna Math Chennai.
