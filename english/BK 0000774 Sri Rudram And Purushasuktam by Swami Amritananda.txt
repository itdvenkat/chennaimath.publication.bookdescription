Translated by Swami Amritananda Sri Rudram and Purusha Suktam are important texts of Vedic mantras. These hymns are addressed to the Universal Being called Rudra and Purusha.The mantras are explained by the translator through the commentaries of Sayana Vishnu Suri Skanda Swami and Abhobala.The text includes the original Sanskrit word-for-word meaning and brief notes from classic Sanskrit commentaries.
