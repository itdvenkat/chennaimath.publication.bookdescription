The Gospel of Sri Ramakrishna is the English translation of the Sri Ramakrishna Kathamrita, the conversations of Sri Ramakrishna with his disciples, devotees, and visitors, recorded by Mahendranath Gupta, one of the intimate disciples of Sri Ramakrishna who wrote the book (under the pseudonym of "M") originally in Bengali language.
