This book is based on the tape-recording of an address given by Swami Ranganathnanda at a luncheon meeting to a cosmopolitan gathering organized in his honour by Smt. &amp; Shri Velji Kothari of Tokyo in the lecture hall of the American Club Tokyo on 7th May 1986. Originally this book was published by Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan Mumbai-400 007 in three editions. This is a revised edition of the book.
