'In The Lap of The Himalayas,' Swami Akhandananda conveys his experiences of the Himalayan pilgrimages. Swami Akhandananda, popularly known as Gangadhar Maharaj and as Baba, was a direct Sannyasin disciple of the Great Master, Sri Ramakrishna. From 1887 till 1897, he was for almost about ten years a wandering pilgrim, visiting nearly all the famous Himalayan Pilgrim centers. Thus, the present volume gives the Swami's experiences and reflections in the Himalayas and the great pilgrimage centers located on that holy mountain.
