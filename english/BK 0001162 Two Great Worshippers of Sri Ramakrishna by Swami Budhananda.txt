Publishing Date: July 2012
Swami Ramakrishnananda's 150th Birthday (July 17, 2012) Publication.
An exposition of how two great disciples- Swami Vivekananda and Swami Ramakrishnananda of Sri Ramakrishna worshiped him in two different ways.
Book Review:
Swami Vivekananda said that whatever he preached he had learnt from Sri Ramakrishna. Swami Ramakrishnananda, another great disciple of Sri Ramakrishna, remarked that whatever he preached, also, was derived from Sri Ramakrishna. Swami Ramakrishnananda said that Sri Ramakrishna had left a ?little message for him?. Indeed, both these illustrious disciples of Sri Ramakrishna worshipped and broadcast the message of their Master in their own way. This booklet discusses various aspects of these two modes of worship
