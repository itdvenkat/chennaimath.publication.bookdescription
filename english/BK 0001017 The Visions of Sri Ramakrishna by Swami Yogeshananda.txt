This book is a collection of a number of super-sensuous experience of the Great Master in the order of their occurance in his life. The present book is a attempt to bring many of the sublime experiences that made him the Great Teacher that he was. Such a book is bound to be of immense help both to spiritual aspirants and to students of comparative mysticism. More over this book is a support to many seekers who are passing through depression and darkness to bring new hope and enlightenment in their spiritual life.
