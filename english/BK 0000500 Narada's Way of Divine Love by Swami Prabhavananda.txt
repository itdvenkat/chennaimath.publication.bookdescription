Narada is one of the great exponents of Bhakti Yoga. He explains that the path of devotion is the easiest way to attain God for it is a path of love. Narada further explains what love is and how it leads us to God.In this edition, the Author Swami Prabhavananda have incorporated the text of Narada Bhakti Sutras in Devanagari as also in English transliteration for the convenience of those readers who are not very familiar with the Devanagari script.
