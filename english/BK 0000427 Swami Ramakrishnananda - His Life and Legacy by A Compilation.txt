

Being a direct disciple of Sri Ramakrishna, Swami Ramakrishnananda popularly known as Shashi Maharaj worked tirelessly to spread the message of Sri Ramakrishna and the ideal of renunciation and service in different parts of South India. His unparallel devotion to Sri Ramakrishna, pure life, saintly personality and love and concern for others are objects of meditation. This volume aims at providing his life and teachings in a concise and handy form for the benefit of greater number of people.
