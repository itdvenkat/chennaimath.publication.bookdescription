What is life in the monasteries of Ramakrishna Order in India? This is a question which many devotees and admirers of the Ramakrishna Movement ask and seek the answer. They want to know how the monks live, how they solve their complicated problems of daily life, with what attitude, and how. And what kind of challenges come to them; could they too have to face problems? This book tries to give a peep into all these.
