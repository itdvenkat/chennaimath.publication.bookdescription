Publishing Date: July 2012

Swami Ramakrishnananda's 150th Birthday Publication - 15-17 July 2012.

This little booklet gives a brief background to the Ramakrishnananda Mandapam, newly inaugurated at Sri Ramakrishna Math, Chennai as part of 150th Birthday celebrations, and also gives brief description of the important aspects of the Mandapam.
