This is a concise English version of the Tamil Saivite scripture Tiruvilaiyadal Puranam which was composed by Paranjyothi Munivar and it is one of the triad of Saivite Puranas in Tamil.

This Puranam narrates 64 divine episodes through which Lord Siva bestowed His grace on devotees.
All 64 episodes are given with pictures drawn by S.Rajam. The colorful one page - pictures explain the situations well. The line drawings speak of the story in detail.

The stories are written by Sri. R. Rangaswamy in simple chaste Tamil which have been translated into English in this Book. Relevant songs of the original work are also given to add to the interest of the reader.
A novel effort to take the Puranam to the younger generation. A treasure for every home and library.
