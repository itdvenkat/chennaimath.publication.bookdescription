
Today's youth is at crossroads with several choices before him or her. One can choose to become great or remain mediocre. One can choose to build one's character with great care and honesty or one can choose to leave it unattened and uncared. At this crucial stage when one has to make abundant choices nothing is of greater importance than getting appropriate guidelines at right time. Keeping this in view Swami Nirvedananda a scholarly monk of the Ramakrishna Order composed verses in Sanskrit delineating certain vows that outline the essence of how to lay foundation for a strong and pure character.These vows represents the cardinal teachings of Swami Vivekananda whose glowing words of strength and purity are a beacon light to all youths.
