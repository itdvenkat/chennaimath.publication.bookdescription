The reader can find the exact details regarding the dates and places when Holy Mother Sri Sarada Devi visited South India. This also describes the period for which she sojourned there, the names of those who accompanied in her trip, the various spiritually significant events that took place and the impression of holiness she left on the hearts of the devotees of the south. Many facts not so far recorded are revealed by Swami Prabhananda's researches, enable us to have a richer picture of this uncommon personality.
