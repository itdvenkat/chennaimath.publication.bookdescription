The articles contained herein were originally contributed to the 1981 Annual Number of the Vedanta Kesari. All these articles included in this book "Yoga: Its Various Aspects" are based on not only theoretical knowledge but also practical experience of the writers in this sublime field. This symposium also highlights some of the more well-tested paths of Yoga and provides detailed instructions regarding what to do and what not to do. Hence these essays will help the earnest readers to attain efficiency and harmony in outer life and profound peace within.
