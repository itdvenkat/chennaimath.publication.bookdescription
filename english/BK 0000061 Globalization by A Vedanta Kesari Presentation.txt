'Globalization' was the theme for 1999December Issue of Vedanta Kesari. This issue was well received by Indian and Foreign readers. This was not only because it contained some outstanding articles from renowned authors and authorities in various disciplines but also because of the contemporary relevance of the subject. The original issue is edited and brought out in a book form for a wider circulation.
Includes articles by Swami Ranganathananda Swami Gautamananda C.Subramaniam Dr.Karan Singh Sri Dayamata Sri K. Dhammananda Valson Thampu Vamadeva Shastri Azgar Ali Engineer Acharya Mahaprajna P.Parameswaran C.T. Kurien Mel Gurtov and other eminent people.
