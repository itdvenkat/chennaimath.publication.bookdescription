Stories and Parables occupy a most important place in the teachings of the saints and seers as the allegorical method of presentation, an effective way of spiritual instruction. Sri Ramakrishna, one of the  greatest spiritual teachers, takes his stories drawn from ordinary domestic and social life, customary with the people who lived arround him. This book for children presents 17 stories and parables of Sri Ramakrishna in a simple language with colorful illustrations. The moral of each story is also explained at the end for the benefit of the kids.
