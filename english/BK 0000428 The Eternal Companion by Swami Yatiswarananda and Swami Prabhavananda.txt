Swami Brahmananda was considered by Sri Ramakrishna as his spiritual son. He was the first President of the Ramakrishna Order and beacon-light of inspiration to all during the infancy of the Ramakrishna Movement. His life and teachings continue to inspire earnest people to new heights of spirituality. The first part of this book contains Swami Brahnananda's biography, and the second part, his teachings.
