Swami Vivekananda came as a dynamic force in the rise of a New India. Swamiji's ideas on education aim at a wholesome development of the personality of man and not a mere accumulation of facts. He believed in a man-making and character-building education. This booklet brings under one cover his stimulating ideas on education. This book will carry the massage of the Master to the thousands of teachers, students and others who are engaged in the great task of education and other nation building activities.
