

This book contains elevating and Invigorating Verses on Divinity, Purity, Strength and Guidelines for Higher Living.

Everyone needs guidance for solving one's problems with clarity and objectivity. And the practice of prayer is a time-test method in this regard. This compilation of soul-stirring prayers from the Sanskrit literature aims to introduce the youth to the practice of prayer for self-development and spiritual living. Being prayerful helps one live a life of fearlessness and strength drawn from a Divine Source.
