The Bhagavad-Gita is the Gospel of Hinduism, and one of the great religious classics of the world. This book by Swami Prabhavananda and Christopher Isherwood is an interpretation of the Gita where they have used the commentaries of Shankara, Sridhara Swami and Madhusudana Saraswati and also Essays on the Gita by Sri Aurobindo. This book serves as a unique reference in the study of Gita.
