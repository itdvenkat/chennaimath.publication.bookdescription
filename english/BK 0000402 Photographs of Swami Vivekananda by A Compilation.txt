In this present volume, a comprehensive collection of photographs of Swami Vivekananda is presented to the reader that will help to visualize Swamiji by seeing him pictured in various moods and poses under diverse contexts and times. All the known photographs available of Swamiji from 1886 to 1901 are included. Almost every photograph is accompanied by a note, which gives its date and place as well as the context in which it was taken.
