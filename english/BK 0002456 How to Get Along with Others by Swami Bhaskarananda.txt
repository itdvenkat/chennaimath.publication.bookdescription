This book deals with the important issue of getting along with others in this stress-laden world and has provided practical suggestions and guidance, acquainting the readers with the ancient Hindu wisdom and stimulating them to use common sense as well in happily getting along with others, at home, at work, or anywhere else.
The author of the book, Swami Bhaskarananda, is a senior monk and the president of The Vedanta Society of Western Washington, Seattle, and the spiritual head of The Vedanta Society in Hawaii and the Vedanta Society in Vancouver.
