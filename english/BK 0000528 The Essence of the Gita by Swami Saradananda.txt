This book contains a set of six talks given by Swami Saradananda, an eminent disciple of Sri Ramakrishna originally in Bengali. All these talks are translated by Swami Tyagananda which present a brilliant summary of the Gita's teaching and the essence of Gita. Hence, these talks, all extempore, are full of insights vital for a healthy spiritual growth. If the reader grasp the essence of this book properly, their study of the complete of the Gita will be more fruitful.
