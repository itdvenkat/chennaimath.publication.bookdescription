Rameshwaram and Kanyakumari are two of the most well-known pilgrimage centres in south India. For centuries, they have been sources of inspiration to countless pilgrims. This book is a travelogue to these two places of pilgrimage with their legends and the religious and historical significance especially in relation with Swami Vivekananda, Holy Mother Sri Sarada Devi, and other disciples of Sri Ramakrishna.
