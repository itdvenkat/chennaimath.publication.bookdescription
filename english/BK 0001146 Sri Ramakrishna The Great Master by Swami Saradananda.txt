Sri Ramakrishna the Great Master translated by Swami Jagadananda, is an English translation of the Bengali biography Sri Ramakrishna Lilaprasanga, of Sri Ramakrishna Paramahamsa, the 19th-century Indian saint and mystic. Its Bengali original was written by Swami Saradananda (a monastic disciple of Sri Ramakrishna) based on interviews of persons who knew him or interacted with him and is therefore the first hand source of information.It combines a biography with a lucid study of the various areas of religion, mysticism and philosophy existing in India and elsewhere from the prehistoric times down to the modern era.
