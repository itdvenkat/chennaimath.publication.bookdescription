
Swami Ramakrishnananda, a direct disciple of Sri Ramakrishna worked tirelessly to spread the message of Sri Ramakrishna and Vedanta through lectures and wide travels which took him to various parts of South India. His deep spirituality was a source of strength and inspiration to all those who came in touch with him. His genuine love and concern for others, his profound scholarship and unshakable faith and steadiness and his practical counsels for facing life's challenges is reflected in the numerous letters he wrote. This book contains most of his letters which will bring succor and strength to all those who are aspiring to lead higher and nobler lives.
