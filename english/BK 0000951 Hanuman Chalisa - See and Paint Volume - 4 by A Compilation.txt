Hanuman Chalisa: See and Paint - 4: This is 4th book in theSee and Paint series of books for Children.

Hanuman Chalisa is the theme of thisSee and Paint book and contains 43 verses and corresponding drawings/pictures to be painted by the Children.

Each drawing page has the verse in Sanskrit with English meaning and the Child is expected to write the sloka its meaning in the space given on the adjacent page and paint the drawing/picture in the vacant outline of the picture.
