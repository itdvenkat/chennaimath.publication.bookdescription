'Search after Happiness' is a revised edition of the books 'The Message of Eternal Wisdom' and 'The Ancient Quest'. This book contains lectures of Swami Ramakrishnananda which he delivered during the years 1893-1911. It is hoped that lovers of Vedantic literature will extend a hearty welcome to this volume embodying the thoughts of a great disciple of Sri Ramakrishna on the eternal wisdom of life.
