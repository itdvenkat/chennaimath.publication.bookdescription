A Special edition with new content and findings. Gives Swami Ramakrishnananda's life, his work, selected reminiscences by devotees and monastics, some of his sayings and a few of his important letters.
While retaining the contents of the previous editions, photographs have been added in this edition to have a visual impact on the reader. In addition, few important letters of Swami Ramakrishnananda have been included to give a better view of his spiritual thoughts and circumstances under which he was working.
The book thus provides in one volume all recorded information about, and available literature on this great disciples of Sri Ramakrishna.
