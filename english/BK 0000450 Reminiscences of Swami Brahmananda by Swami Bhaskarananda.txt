Swami Brahmananda, a direct disciple of Bhagavan Sri Ramakrishna has left behind a rich legacy of how to live a spiritually vibrant life. His lofty state of mind, practical wisdom and a deep love and sympathy for all those who came in touch with him, is a legand in the Ramakrishna tradition. This book is a translation from Bengali, Brahmananda Leelakatha, as the original book is titled, was written by Brahmachari Akshaya chaitanya. Swami Bhaskarananda, the translator of the present volume has taken great pain to provide a faithful and clear translation. This book will serve as a manual of practical spirituality.
