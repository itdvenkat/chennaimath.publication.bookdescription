Swami Gnaneswarananda the founder of the Vivekananda Vedanta Society in Chicago has taken enormous pains to explain the essence of traditional yoga in a concise lucid and lovely language shorn of all technicalities in his book "Yoga for Beginners". His up-to-date phraseology laced with humour can be understood and enjoyed by all enquiring minds.
