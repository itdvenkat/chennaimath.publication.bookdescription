This book containing choice prayers from select hymns presents to the devotee just a little cream of devotion from the mighty milk oceanof Sanskrit religious literature. The lines are free sectarian bias and speak of the universal aspects of the Deity common to all religionsand creeds. As such they are sure to appeal to all seekers after Truth who follow the path of devotion in some form or other and wantpassages for meditation praise and prayer all together.
