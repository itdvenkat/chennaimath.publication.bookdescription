This is an attempt to place of the central ideas of the Gita in a student's perspective. It is a topical selection of Gita verses keeping a student in mind. Swami Vivekananda's quotes have been appropriately added at the end of each verse. A few thematic pictures in between the pages add visual impact to this book.

The book makes an easy reading; it is a ready-reference to know what Gita says about control of mind, overcoming anger, concentration of mind, healthy interpersonal relationships, true happiness and so on.
