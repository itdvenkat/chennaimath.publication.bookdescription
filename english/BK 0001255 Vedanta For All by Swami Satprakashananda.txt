Swami Satprakashananda an eminent scholar monk of Ramakrishna Math and Mission is known for his yeomen service through his lectures articles and books to spread. The present volume is a collection of Swami Satprakashananda's class talks on different aspects of Vedanta. He discusses the why and wherefore of morality the role of the spiritual teacher the aura of meditation the dichotomy of good and evil and the reconciliation between Divine Grace and the Law of Karma. He explores also the enigma of Maya the interrelation between reason volition and emotion visions the power of mantras and the million dollar question of what happens after death. In fine the book is not only 'Vedanta for All' also 'All about Vedanta'. A diligent study fo this book is bound to provide satisfying solutions to all the problems of the reader material moral and spiritual.
