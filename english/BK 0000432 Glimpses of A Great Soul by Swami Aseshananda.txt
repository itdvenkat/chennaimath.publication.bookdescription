Swami Saradananda was a direct disciple of Sri Ramakrishna. A man of high spiritual attainments, the Swami, was known for his profound devotion to Holy Mother Sri Sarada Devi, genuine concern for others, plain common sense and his extraordinary serenity of mind. This book is an intimate portrait of the personality of Swami Saradananda, giving many details of his personal life and dealings which will inspire a greater number of spiritual seekers and admirers of the Ramakrishna Order.
