Dohavali is an important work by the famous saint-poet Goswami Tulasidas. It is written in Awadhi language as two lined verses named dohas and a few sorathas. It contains both spiritual and secular wisdom with specific emphasis on devotion to Sri Rama and chanting Rama Nama.
This book is an English translation of Dohavali by Swami Brahmeshananda, a senior monk of the Ramakrishna Order who is a former editor of The Vedanta Kesari and the author of many popular books.
