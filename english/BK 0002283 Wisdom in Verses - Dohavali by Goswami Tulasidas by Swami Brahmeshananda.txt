

This book is the English translation of Dohavali, an important work in Awadhi language by the famous saint-poet Goswami Tulasidas, which contains both spiritual and secular wisdoms, with specific emphasis on the devotion to Sri Rama and chanting Rama Nama.

Swami Brahmeshananda, a senior monk of the Ramakrishna Order who is a former Editor of The Vedanta Kesari and the author of many popular books, has translated the text and given explanatory notes.
