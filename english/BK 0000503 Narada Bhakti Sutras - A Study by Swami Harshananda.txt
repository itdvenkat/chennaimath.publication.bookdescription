Sri Ramakrishna declared that for this Age of Kali-- the Iron Age--when people are very much attached to their bodies and hence to physical pleasures, the simple path of bhakti or devotion taught by Narada is the most efficacious path to perfection. This brochure is a systematic summary of the well-known work of Narada--translated from the original Kannada version entitled Narada Bhakti Sutragalu--Ondu Adhyayana.
